you need to know
  • The property has connecting/adjoining rooms, which are subject to availability and can be requested by contacting the property using the number on the booking confirmation.
  • This property advises that enhanced cleaning and guest safety measures are currently in place
  • Disinfectant is used to clean the property; commonly-touched surfaces are cleaned with disinfectant between stays; bed sheets and towels are laundered at a temperature of at least 60°C/140°F.

  • Social distancing measures are in place; staff at the property wear personal protective equipment; a shield is in place between staff and guests in main contact areas; periodic temperature checks are conducted on staff; temperature checks are available to guests; guests are provided with hand sanitizer; masks are required in public areas.

  • Individually-wrapped food options are available for breakfast and through room service.

Hotel description

Isn’t it all about enjoying your holiday! Hilton Worldwide's property is budget-friendly, where you don’t need to break open your piggy bank to enjoy yourself and light it up.
In Alexandria you will be near to Stabler-Leadbeater Apothecary Museum, Air Force Memorial, and many more places when you stay at Embassy Suites Alexandria - Old Town, which is few steps away from all the pleasurable sightseeing.
Hospitality, warm reception, and friendly staff are what you will find when you first step into the property not only because of the warm atmosphere, but also for recreational services excellence which embodied in having a fitness centre and an indoor pool. The accommodations are rigged up with air conditioning, microwaves and telephones.

Check-in: From 03:00 PM

Check-out: Until 12:00 PM

loaction
Located in Alexandria (Old Town Alexandria), Embassy Suites Hotel Alexandria Old Town is within a 10-minute drive of MGM National Harbor and Fashion Center at Pentagon City. This hotel is 6.4 mi (10.2 km) from Arlington National Cemetery and 7.6 mi (12.2 km) from Vietnam Veterans Memorial.
dining
Feast your senses at one of Embassy Suites Alexandria - Old Town's eateries serving Mediterranean cuisines, or tranquillise with a drink at the property's lounge. Room service is also available around the clock.
rooms
Wake up in the morning and enjoy the atmosphere of Alexandria at one of 288 comfortable accommodations at Embassy Suites Alexandria - Old Town where every detail was carefully selected and quality crafted for the guests, featuring a refrigerator, heating and a seating area and for more pleasure, there is also a TV with cable channels in addition to an iron. When it comes to bathing, you can have a relaxing one in your private bathroom that is fitted with a shower and towels. Housekeeping services are provided as well.
Amenities
Embassy Suites Alexandria - Old Town provides a group of magnificent services to meet guests’ needs, such as the fitness centre and the indoor pool.
Business, and other amenities
Featured amenities include wired Internet access (surcharge), a 24-hour business center, and complimentary newspapers in the lobby. Planning an event in Alexandria? This hotel has facilities measuring 6234 square feet (579 square meters), including conference space. Self parking (subject to charges) is available onsite.
